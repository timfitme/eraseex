Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025  ·  Smart Eraser – Element Hider & Page Cleaner

Short version: Smart Eraser does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All your rules are stored locally on your device. The only external request is license key validation when you activate Premium.

1. What data we collect

Smart Eraser collects no personal data. The extension does not track browsing history, does not read page content beyond what is needed to hide selected elements, and does not send any information to third-party analytics or advertising services.

2. Local storage

All data the extension creates is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. This includes:

  • CSS selectors of elements you chose to hide (per-domain and global rules)
  • Your Premium status flag (isPremium)
  • Your license key (stored locally so you don't have to re-enter it)
  • Enabled Cleaner settings (YouTube, Reddit, Facebook, etc.)
  • Selected UI theme preference

This data never leaves your device except as described in Section 3.

3. License key validation

When you activate a Premium license, the extension sends your license key to our validation server (eraseex-beta.vercel.app), which verifies it against the LemonSqueezy payment platform. No other personal information is transmitted. The server does not log or store your key beyond the duration of the request.

4. Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save your hidden-element rules locally
  • activeTab — to interact with the currently active browser tab
  • scripting — to inject the content script that hides elements
  • tabs — to read the current tab's URL so rules are applied per-domain
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — required to run the content script on any website you visit

5. Third-party services

Premium purchases are processed by LemonSqueezy. Please refer to their Privacy Policy for information about how they handle payment data.

6. Children's privacy

Smart Eraser is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at the extension's Chrome Web Store listing page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support tab on the extension listing page.
